You have selected to apply for Medicaid Assistance.

Medical Assistance programs provide medical coverage for adults, children, pregnant women, elderly and disabled people. Medical coverage may help pay medical bills, doctor's visits and Medicare premiums. Individuals who are not eligible for Medical Assistance may be automatically referred to the Health Insurance Marketplace.

If you want to apply for other programs (TANF, Food Assistance, Child Services, Energy Assistance) you can do so at the end of this application.
